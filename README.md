# NavTag

NavTag is revolutionizing how tolls are paid throughout the United States with an innovative, License plate-based system. Our application eliminates using cash, RFID transponders, or any physical hardware while offering drivers a seamless and contactless solution. Registering your license plate and government ID opens you to 49 states' access to their toll systems. NavTag offers a very intuitive mobile app for account management and funding by credit or debit cards. It is highly secured with a state-of-the-art location-based verification system that can ensure the authenticity of the transactions within a 2-kilometer radius of toll points. Our platform seamlessly integrates with the existing toll infrastructure and unlike our competitors, NavTag can be used throughout the nation. NavTag is not just solving today's problems, we aim to pave the way for the future of smart, connected transportation infrastructure. By streamlining the process of toll payments and reducing traffic congestion by implementing contactless payments, we are also contributing to environmental sustainability efforts. NavTag can also be expanded beyond toll systems and implemented in parking spaces. With NavTag, goodbye to the struggles of traditional toll payments as we enter a contactless, nationwide tolling.
